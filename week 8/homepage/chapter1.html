Chapter 1: The Spark

The first chapter of the book. It introduces the main character, Shlok Italiya,
In the bustling city of Surat, India, lived a young computer engineering student named Shlok Italiya.
From a young age, I was fascinated by the world of technology. Despite not being from a well-developed country, the internet became a gateway to endless possibilities.
The spark ignited when I wrote the first line of code, realizing the power to create and solve problems.
